Ludo Master: Elevating the Classic Ludo Experience

"Ludo Master," crafted by the innovative Santosh Adhikari from Pokhara, Nepal, is not just another digital board game. It's an evolution of the ancient Indian game Ludo into a digital masterpiece, offering a revitalized and enriched gaming experience. This adaptation brings the beloved traditional Ludo board game into the digital era, catering to modern players while preserving the essence of the classic gameplay.
